# Digital Open Learning

## Description

Digital Open Learning is an open and collaborative online learning community space and a living repository.

The platform is being designed for those new to and curious about the world of digital as well as those who are already actively working in the digital domain. It will support those who may want to improve their skills in a new subject area or deepen their knowledge on a given topic.

This platform will be open to anyone, including Canadian public servants across Canada at the federal, provincial/territorial, and municipal governments in keeping with the FPT Declaration on Public Sector Innovation.

It will support continuous learning, career development and knowledge transfer across public and private sectors.
